# Teacher Onboarding

After creating your account and verifying your email, you'll go through a quick two-step onboarding process to set up your tinyverse experience. This helps personalize the platform for your location and language preferences.

### Step 1: Choose your language

1. Select your preferred interface language from the dropdown
2. The interface will update immediately to show you the new language
3. Click **Next** to continue

Your language choice sets the default for your entire interface. You can [change this later](#06-language-theme-settings) if needed.

📷 *Screenshot: Step 1 showing the language selection dropdown with available languages*

### Step 2: Select your country and school

1. **Choose your country** from the searchable list
2. **Find or create your school:**
   - Start typing to search for your school in the list
   - If your school appears, select it
   - If your school isn't listed, keep typing to create a new one — it will be added when you finish
   - Check "I'm not associated with a school" if you're working independently

3. Click **Finish** to complete onboarding

📷 *Screenshot: Step 2 showing country selection and school search/create interface*

> [!info]
> If you create a new school, it will be added to the tinyverse database and become available for other teachers in your country to select.

> [!tip]
> Don't worry about getting everything perfect — you can always update your [preferred language](#06-language-theme-settings) and school selection later from the [Settings page](#101-teacher-profile-settings).

Once you finish onboarding, you'll be redirected to the tinyverse dashboard where you can start creating experiments and classes.
