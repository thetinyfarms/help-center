# What is the tinyverse?

The tinyverse is an interactive science platform that connects real-world smart devices — like plant growth chambers with sensors, cameras, and LED lights — to a web-based dashboard. Teachers create experiments, students collect data, and everyone learns by doing.

Whether you're monitoring temperature, photographing plant growth over time, or programming automated watering schedules, the tinyverse brings hands-on STEM learning into the classroom. Students don't just read about science — they experiment, observe, and discover.

### Key features

- **For teachers:** Design experiments, manage classes, assign class projects, and track student progress from one dashboard
- **For students:** Join a class with a simple code, run experiments, collect real sensor data, collaborate with classmates, and present findings
- **Smart devices:** Control and stream live sensor data (temperature, humidity, CO₂, light) from connected devices over WiFi
- **Visual programming:** Students can write visual programs to automate device actions — like watering plants or adjusting lights
- **Real-time data:** Monitor experiments with live sensor readings, charts, photos, and timelapses

### Who is it for

- **Teachers** who want to bring interactive, data-driven science experiments to their classrooms
- **Students** of all ages who learn best by doing hands-on experiments
- **Schools** looking for engaging STEM education tools that connect digital and physical learning
