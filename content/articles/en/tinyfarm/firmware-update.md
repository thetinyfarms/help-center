# Firmware Updates

Keeping your Tinyfarm firmware up to date ensures you have the latest features and bug fixes.

## How to update

1. Visit the [Tinyfarm device selection](/tinyfarm) page
2. Select your device model (1.0 or 1.5)
3. Connect your Tinyfarm via USB-C
4. Click **Flash Latest Firmware**
5. Wait for the process to complete — do not disconnect during flashing

## What gets updated

- Sensor reading improvements
- WiFi connectivity fixes
- New Tinyverse integration features
- Bug fixes and stability improvements

## After updating

- Your WiFi credentials will be reset — reconfigure them after flashing
- The device will restart automatically
- Check Tinyverse to verify your device reconnects
