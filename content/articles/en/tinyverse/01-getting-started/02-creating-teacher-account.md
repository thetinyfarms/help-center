# Creating a Teacher Account

Getting started with the tinyverse takes just a minute. You can create a teacher account using your email address or sign in instantly with your Google or Microsoft account.

## How to register

1. Go to the [registration page](https://universe.tiny.science/teacher/register)
2. Choose your sign-up method:
   - **With email:** Enter your full name, email address, and create a password (at least 6 characters)
   - **With Google or Microsoft:** Click the corresponding button to sign in with single sign-on
3. If you registered with email, check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account
4. Once verified, you'll be guided through a short [onboarding process](#03-teacher-onboarding) to set your language and school

📷 *Screenshot: The registration page showing email fields and Google/Microsoft sign-in buttons*

## Sign-up options

### Email registration
- Requires a valid email address
- Password must be at least 6 characters
- You'll need to verify your email before accessing your account

### Single sign-on (Google/Microsoft)
- Faster sign-up with no password to remember
- Uses your existing Google or Microsoft account
- No email verification needed

> [!tip]
> If you already have an account, use the "Sign in" link at the bottom of the registration page to log in instead.

> [!warning]
> If you see an error saying your email already exists, you may have already created an account. Try logging in or use the "Forgot password" link if needed.
