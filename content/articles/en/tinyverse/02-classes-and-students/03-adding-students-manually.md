# Adding Students Manually

You can pre-create student accounts for your class instead of having students join themselves. This is useful when you want to control student credentials or set up accounts in advance.

## How to add students manually

1. Go to your class page
2. In the Students section, click the **Add** button
3. Enter a username for the student (will be converted to lowercase)
4. Enter a 4-digit PIN that the student will use to log in
5. Click "Add student"

The student account is created immediately, and you'll see it in the Students section. Share the credentials with the student so they can access their account.

📷 *Screenshot: Add student dialog with username and PIN fields*

> [!info]
> The username only needs to be unique within your class, not across the entire platform. The 4-digit PIN serves as the student's password.

> [!tip]
> Students can also [join your class themselves](#14-how-students-join-class) using the Class ID. Both methods work the same way once the account is created.
