# Language & Theme Settings

The tinyverse supports multiple languages and display themes. You can customize both to match your preferences from two convenient locations.

### Changing your language

1. Click the language selector in the bottom-right corner of any page, or
2. Go to Account settings and find the "Platform" section

Your language preference is saved to your account and will apply across all your devices.

📷 *Screenshot: Language selector dropdown showing available languages*

> [!tip]
> When you first create an account, the tinyverse automatically detects your language based on your browser settings or location. You can change it anytime.

> [!info]
> The tinyverse is available in 7 languages: English, Deutsch (German), Nederlands (Dutch), Lietuvių (Lithuanian), Dansk (Danish), Hrvatski (Croatian), and Malti (Maltese).

### Changing your theme

1. Click the theme toggle (sun/moon icon) in the bottom-right corner of any page, or
2. Go to Account settings and find the "Platform" section

Your theme preference is saved to your account and will apply across all your devices.

📷 *Screenshot: Theme toggle button showing light/dark mode options*

> [!info]
> The tinyverse is available in light and dark mode, with dark mode as default.
