# Account & Settings

Manage your Tinyverse account, notifications, and preferences.

## Changing your password

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account**
2. Click **Change password**
3. Enter your current password and a new one
4. Click **Save**

## Notification preferences

Tinyverse can alert you when something needs attention:

- **Device offline** — a Tinyfarm lost its connection
- **Sensor out of range** — temperature, humidity, or CO₂ outside your targets
- **Experiment milestones** — reminders based on your experiment timeline

Configure these under **Settings** > **Notifications**.

## Language settings

Tinyverse supports multiple languages. Change your language under **Settings** > **Language** or use the language selector in the top bar.

## Deleting your account

Contact support at [support@tinyfarm.com](mailto:support@tinyfarm.com) to request account deletion. All your data will be permanently removed.
