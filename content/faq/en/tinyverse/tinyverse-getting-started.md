# Getting Started with Tinyverse

Tinyverse is your dashboard for monitoring and managing Tinyfarm devices.

## Creating an account

1. Go to [tinyverse.live](https://tinyverse.live)
2. Click **Sign up** and enter your email
3. Verify your email address
4. Log in to your new dashboard

## Adding your first device

Once your Tinyfarm is powered on and connected to WiFi, it will automatically appear in your Tinyverse dashboard. No pairing code or manual setup is needed — just make sure your device has internet access.

## Dashboard overview

- **Devices** — see all your connected Tinyfarms and their live status
- **Experiments** — create and manage growing experiments
- **Data** — view historical sensor readings and charts
- **Settings** — manage your account, notifications, and preferences
